Encourage Golf

Encourage Golf is an inclusive, community-driven golf program based in Anamosa, Iowa. We proudly welcome school-aged children and beginner adults who are ready to learn, grow, and have fun through the game of golf.

Whether you’re picking up a club for the first time or looking to build a solid foundation, our EG Camps are designed to make golf approachable, enjoyable, and rewarding for everyone.

Golf is a sport you can play for life—so why not start now? At Encourage Golf, we’re not just teaching the fundamentals; we’re growing a lifelong love for the game.

Our Team

Encourage Golf, founded by lifelong friends and former Iowa High School State Golf Champs, Brian Neville and Sean Smith, is committed to fostering a love for the game in players of all skill levels. Through our inclusive golf lessons, we aim to teach not only the fundamentals of golf, but also valuable life skills. Our mission is to inspire confidence, perseverance, and sportsmanship in every participant, creating a supportive community where everyone can thrive both on and off the course.

Join the Encourage Golf Team Today!

Ready to take your golf game to the next level? Encourage Golf Camps are the perfect place for school-aged kids and beginner adults to build their skills in a fun, supportive environment. Whether you’re new to the game or looking to sharpen your swing, our team is here to help every step of the way.

Why Choose Encourage Golf?

  • Personalized Coaching: Low student-to-coach ratio (typically 2:1 or 3:1)

  • Top-Tier Facility: Practice all parts of your game, including a massive short-game area

  • Cutting-Edge Technology: Our simulator captures video and swing data to help track progress

  • Flexible Scheduling: More availability on the horizon—including summer sessions!

Duration: 75-minute sessions (please arrive 10 minutes early for stretching and paperwork)

Group Size: Small groups of up to 6 students with 2–4 coaches assisting each session

Frequency: Multiple days available each week
